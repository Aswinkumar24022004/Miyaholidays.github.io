Detailed Tour Descriptions

Get an in-depth look at what each of our exciting tour packages offers.

Valley Tour

Embark on a scenic journey through Kodaikanal's most breathtaking valleys and viewpoints. This tour is perfect for those who wish to capture stunning panoramic views and experience the serene beauty of the region.

Highlights:

  • Upper Lake View: A mesmerizing view of Kodaikanal Lake from an elevated perspective.
  • Moier Point: Enjoy the vast expanse of the plains and distant hills.
  • Pine Tree Forest: Wander through dense pine groves, a popular spot for photography.
  • Guna Cave (Devil's Kitchen): Explore the mysterious caves, known for their unique rock formations.
  • Pillar Rock: Witness the majestic three giant rock pillars standing shoulder to shoulder.
  • Golf Course: A picturesque green expanse offering tranquility.
  • Green Valley View: Another spectacular viewpoint offering deep valley views.
  • Liril Falls: A charming waterfall, especially vibrant during monsoon.
  • Shopping Point: Time for local souvenirs and handicrafts.
  • Coaker's Walk: A paved pedestrian path offering stunning views of the plains.
  • Bryant Park: A botanical garden with a rich variety of flowers and plants.
  • Kodaikanal Lake Drop: Conclude your tour at the iconic Kodaikanal Lake.

Duration: Approximately 4-5 hours.

Village Tour (Shooting Point)

Experience the rustic charm of Kodaikanal's surrounding villages and discover popular filming locations. This tour offers a glimpse into the local life and stunning natural backdrops.

Highlights:

  • Pine Forest (Shooting Point): Famous for its cinematic beauty and tranquil environment.
  • Palani View: Offers a distant view of the Palani plains and temples.
  • Mahalakshmi Temple: A serene spiritual stop.
  • Poomparai Village View: A panoramic view of the quaint Poomparai village nestled in the hills.
  • Kulanthai Velappar Temple (3000 Years Old): Visit an ancient temple with rich history.
  • Sheep Farm: A delightful stop, especially for families.
  • Manavanur Lake: A serene lake offering boating and picturesque surroundings.
  • Kodaikanal Lake / Or City Drop: End your tour at the lake or city center.

Duration: Approximately 5-6 hours.

Trekking Tour

For the adventurous souls, our trekking tour takes you deep into Kodaikanal's natural wonders, offering exhilarating trails and breathtaking discoveries. Get ready to connect with nature!

Highlights:

  • La Saleth Church: A beautiful church with unique architecture and serene surroundings.
  • Vattakanal Falls: A refreshing waterfall, perfect for a short break.
  • Mountain Beauty: Trek through lush green mountains with stunning vistas.
  • Dolphin's Nose: A unique rock formation resembling a dolphin's nose, offering spectacular views.
  • Echo Rock: Experience the natural echo phenomenon.
  • 500 Year Old Tree: Marvel at the ancient, majestic tree.
  • Shopping Point: A brief stop for local essentials or souvenirs.
  • Kodaikanal Lake (Or) City Drop: Conclude your trekking adventure back in town.

Duration: Varies based on trail difficulty (approx. 4-7 hours).

Forest Tour

Immerse yourself in the tranquility of Kodaikanal's dense forests. This tour requires prior permission and offers an exclusive experience of untouched natural beauty and serene landscapes.

Highlights:

  • Silent Valley (Echo Point): A peaceful valley known for its echoing properties.
  • Fire Watching Tower: Get a bird's-eye view of the forest canopy.
  • Berijam Lake View: A stunning viewpoint overlooking the pristine Berijam Lake.
  • Caps Fly View: Another unique viewpoint with captivating scenery.
  • Mathikettan Forest: Explore the mystical and dense forest.
  • Berijam Lake: A protected lake, offering unparalleled natural beauty (permission required for entry).
  • Kodaikanal Lake / or City Drop: Return to Kodaikanal after your forest adventure.

(Forest Permission Must: Please arrange in advance for this tour.)

Duration: Approximately 6-8 hours.

Park Tour

Discover the cultivated beauty and serene green spaces of Kodaikanal with our park tour. Ideal for families and those who enjoy leisurely strolls amidst nature and local cultural sites.

Highlights:

  • Jain Temple: A tranquil place of worship with beautiful architecture.
  • Kurunji Andavar Temple: Dedicated to Lord Muruga, offering panoramic views of the plains.
  • Palani View: Another vantage point to admire the distant Palani Hills.
  • Kodaikanal City View: Get a bird's-eye perspective of the charming Kodaikanal town.
  • Chettiar Park: A small, well-maintained park known for its beautiful flowers.
  • Natural Science Museum: Explore the local flora, fauna, and geological exhibits.
  • Silver Cascade Falls: A picturesque waterfall formed by the overflow of Kodaikanal Lake.
  • Bryant Park: A renowned botanical garden, perfect for a relaxing walk.
  • Kodaikanal Lake (Or) City Drop: End your tour at the iconic lake or city center.

Duration: Approximately 3-4 hours.
